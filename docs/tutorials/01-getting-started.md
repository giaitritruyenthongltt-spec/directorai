# 01 — Getting Started (3 minutes)

**Goal**: viewer goes from a fresh install to one successful
DirectorAI-driven cut on the `hello-vlog` sample. No prior context.

## Cold open (0:00 – 0:10)

> "If you've ever wished Premiere could do the boring cuts for you,
> stick around — three minutes from now you'll have a finished cut
> with one click."

Show the empty Premiere timeline; the panel sidebar is collapsed.

## Step 1 — Install & connect (0:10 – 0:45)

- Show the MSI installer running (P4.24).
- Open Premiere → Window → Extensions → DirectorAI.
- Highlight the status bar: green dot, project name, sequence name.

> "If your status bar shows the green dot you're already wired up."

## Step 2 — Load the sample (0:45 – 1:15)

- File → Import → `samples/hello-vlog/media/intro.mp4` (P4.32 sample).
- Drag the clip onto the timeline.
- Switch back to the panel.

## Step 3 — Apply a style (1:15 – 2:20)

- Click **Style** tab.
- Pick **vlog** from the dropdown.
- Click **Dry-run** — narrate the plan output. "14 steps: silence
  trim, two zoom punches, beat-snapped cuts, fade out."
- Click **Apply**. Watch the progress bar (P4.04). Single Ctrl-Z
  rolls everything back if you want.

## Step 4 — Verify (2:20 – 2:45)

- Scrub the timeline. Point out the trimmed silences and the zoom on
  the word "Premiere".
- Toggle the music track; the audio fade is in.

## Close (2:45 – 3:00)

> "That's it — one cut, one style, one minute of your time. Next video
> we'll write your own style file."

End card: `directorai.app`, Discord link, "Building a Style →".
