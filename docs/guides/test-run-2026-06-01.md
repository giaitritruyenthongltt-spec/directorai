## Known Premiere 26 limitations (out of V-set scope)

These were surfaced by the live smoke and are NOT fixed in V1-V5:

1. **`applyTransition`**: Premiere 26 removed both `TransitionFactory`
   and `track.addTransition`. Current adapter throws "no compatible
   API found". Workaround: skip transitions in plans, manual apply.
2. **Lumetri `Component.create('AE.ADBE Lumetri')`**: previously
   observed to hang 30s on the first call after panel load. Cause
   unclear — may be Premiere initialization, may be UXP lockedAccess
   contention. V2's clipCache reduces non-first-call latency but
   doesn't address the first-time hang.
