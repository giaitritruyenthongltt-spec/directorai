(`/press` page) and external press. Until they ship CI will warn.

| ----------------- | ------ | --------------- | -------- |

## Spacing rule

Maintain a clear area equal to the height of the mark on all four
sides when placing the lockup against other content. Don't tint;
keep the original `#4c8bf5` accent unless inverting on a fully
saturated background.

## Don't

- Don't stretch.
- Don't add drop shadows.
- Don't recolor the mark.
- Don't rotate.
