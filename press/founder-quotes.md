# Founder quotes — ready to drop in

Attribute to: **DirectorAI Inc, founding team**.

## On the why

> "Editors don't want one more dropdown that says 'auto-cut'. They
> want a tool that gets _their_ taste, not a generic taste. That's
> the gap DirectorAI tries to close."

## On determinism vs. AI hype

> "Most of what DirectorAI does is a rule planner — deterministic,
> testable, debuggable. The LLM only refines. We use AI where it
> adds judgement, not where determinism is cheaper."

## On running locally

> "Your B-roll, your transcripts, your half-finished podcast about
> someone's startup — none of it should leave your machine. We
> built the whole context engine in Python so you can verify that
> for yourself."

## On the Style Engine

> "The first time DirectorAI suggests a cut that lands exactly the
> way you would have done it manually, that's the moment we know
> we've built the right thing. The learner gets you there in two
> or three correction loops."

## On the beta

> "We ran a closed beta of 20-something creators for two months
> before opening it up. Everything from the cancel button to the
> 7-day offline grace exists because someone in that cohort told
> us we'd break their workflow without it."

## On pricing

> "We picked one-time licenses because creator software you actually
> use should be ownable. The subscription tier is for people who
> want the upcoming style marketplace — that's its own thing."
